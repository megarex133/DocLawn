About Us

The idea to create a device for picking up dog poop was suggested to me by my friend. He has experience of owning several dogs and of course he walks them every day. Spending time with favorite pets is interrupted every time when they leave waste. To remove waste from the lawn, my friend must bend down, and with a plastic bag put on his hand, has to make contact with this unpleasant smelling sticky and slippery "substance". He assures me that even with careful use of the sealed bag, the smell of waste was still transmitted to his hands. In addition, he is worried about the ecology, pollution of the environment with disposable plastic bags. He asked to come up with a convenient device to solve this double "delicate" problem. And finally, I am pleased to present to your attention the DOCLawn device for picking up dog waste

DOCLawn – dog operation “clean lawn”

The principle of its operation is very simple, and the whole procedure takes about 40 seconds. Just two clicks on the button and no more bending down to pick up waste. A replaceable cardboard cartridge is inserted into the DOCLawn by lightly pressing the hand or simply by pressing the DOCLawn on the cartridge located on the surface of the lawn. All you need to do is to lower the DOCLawn with the cartridge directly on the waste and press the button once. The cartridge will begin to rotate, and due to the low special shape the waste will begin to move from the surface of the lawn directly into the cartridge. To improve the efficiency of waste collecting it is necessary to raise and lower alternately the working DOCLawn on the dog waste. The rotation of the cartridge is stopped by pressing the button again. After collecting the waste, you can continue your walk while holding DOCLawn in your hand. When approaching the waste collection point, you must press the button twice, a special internal mechanism will stop holding the cartridge, and it will fall into the trash can by itself. The waste is removed, and the hands of the person and the cleaning device itself remain clean.

The double “delicate” problem has been resolved

The removable cartridge is 100% regular cardboard, i.e. made of densely pressed layers of paper and PVA glue, the glue that is environmentally friendly and harmless so that even children in schools can use it safely. These cartridges are biodegradable with waste and can be recycled to reduce the amount of plastic used by humans and improve the environment.

DOCLawn – as clean as a sheet of paper.

As a bonus, DOCLawn has an awesome flashlight for cleaning up waste in the dark or for a safe walk. Due to its location on the device, the flashlight illuminates the cleaning area very well. The power of the flashlight is enough to illuminate a large area. Switching on/ switching off occurs by long pressing of the button.

Faq

However, confidence in the success of our project is based on the results of a detailed calculation of the feasibility study and a lot of preparatory work before the start of DOCLawn production, that led to the project's release to fundraising. The materials provided do not include computer graphics or modeling. All videos are real and unprocessed. It is important for us to provide real DOCLawn work without embellishments. Therefore, in tests and final videos, we have always tested it not on bananas or other substitutes, but on the dog feces itself. In addition, in order to protect intellectual property, documents for patent and trademark registration of DOCLawn are submitted in advance.

  • What is the readiness of the device for commercial release?

    The product is ready for serial production. The DOCLawn creator has done a lot of work from the idea to the existing sample. Many tests have been carried out. All electrical components used, except the printed circuit board (PCB), are widespread and available for order from factories around the world. The PCB is custom designed and produced specifically for DOCLawn. Design documentation has been prepared. All of DOCLawn's composite plastic parts are now 3D printed, and have been checked by technical expertise for manufacturing injection molded plastic in production. In addition, we did not waste time and have already designed the molds with engineering documentation and drawings. Negotiations were carried on with all factories, where orders for the production of the necessary parts will be placed. We clearly understand when, how, where and what else will need to be done to tie all these links of one production chain together.

  • When will I receive my DOCLawn if I become a backer?

    We expect to start shipping the first units of DOCLawn to backers approximately 120-140 days after the end of the campaign. This parameter consists of the following components: The order and delivery of the necessary electronic components for assembly; the manufacture of molds for plastic injection at metalworking factories will take about 90 days. The rest of the time will be spent on assembling and preparing DOCLawn for sending to backers. However, increased demand may change the delivery time and schedule. For example, one DOCLawn bill of materials (BOM) includes 41 items of various parts. In order to reduce the time it takes for backers to receive DocLawn, we will start to organize the production and manufacture components as soon as the preliminary order quantity is known, without waiting for the end of the project. The production of replacement cardboard cartridges will begin immediately after the end of the campaign and will take place concurrently with the production of the main unit.

  • How do I manage DOCLawn?

    The principle of operation is very simple, the whole procedure for collecting dog poop takes about 40 seconds and requires only two presses of the button. A replaceable cardboard cartridge is inserted into DOCLawn in advance. This is done by lightly inserting it into the device or simply pressing DOCLawn on the cartridge installed on the surface. After installing the cartridge, you need to lower the DOCLawn directly on the dog waste and press the button once. The cartridge will begin to rotate and dog waste will begin to move from the surface of the lawn directly into the cartridge due to its special shape. To improve the collecting efficiency of the item it is necessary to alternately raise and lower the working DOCLawn on the waste. The rotation of the cartridge is stopped by pressing the button again. After collecting waste, you can continue your walk while holding DOCLawn in your hands. When approaching the waste collection point, you must press the button twice, a special internal mechanism will stop holding the cartridge, and it will fall into the trash can. Dog waste is removed, and the hands of the person and the cleaning device itself remain clean.

  • What device versions do you offer?

    DOCLawn can be used in two versions, a long walking stick or a short umbrella that is held in the hands or worn by the wrist strap. The long-handled DOCLawn protects your back and legs from pain and makes walking easier for people with disabilities. Using any version of DOCLawn will allow you to avoid direct contact with dog waste.

  • How many buttons are there on the device and in what mode do they work?

    DOCLawn has two control buttons. One on the body itself for working in the version of a short umbrella, the second on the handle of the telescopic handle in the version of a long cane. Both buttons are equivalent, duplicate and can function independently of each other. Pressing any button once starts or stops rotation of the replacement cartridge. Pressing the button twice releases the replacement cartridge. Long press of the button turns the flashlight on or off.

  • How will I receive the device?

    The device is delivered in a cardboard box ready-made with two separately located elements: the DOCLawn body and the removed telescopic handle. You can use DOCLawn by removing or attaching the telescopic handle, no additional assembly is required. After receiving DOCLawn, you will need to install two 18650 batteries, each with a capacity of at least 2200 mA. Batteries are not included in the package. This decision was made in order to avoid unnecessary problems with the postal delivery of DOCLawn, since different transport companies have their own safety standards and special restrictions apply to the shipment of batteries.

  • What are the dimensions and weight of the device?

    The height at the end of the telescopic handle is about 32″ (82 cm) and about 25″ (64 cm) at the shortest end. The bottom largest diameter is about 4″ (10 cm). Weight is about 1.76 lbs (0.8 kg). If using the version without a telescopic handle, the height is 30 cm and the weight is about 1.43 lbs (0.65 kg).

  • What material is the device made of?

    DOCLawn is made of polyamide, it is a durable plastic that is more than 2 times stronger than conventional plastic and is more resistant to deterioration, shocks, temperature extremes and aggressive factors.

  • What material is the replacement cartridge made of and what are its dimensions? Is it environmentally friendly?

    The replacement cartridge is 100% cardboard, i.e. made of densely pressed layers of paper and PVA glue, it is environmentally friendly and safe. These cartridges are biodegradable with dog waste and can be recycled so it reduces the amount of plastic used by humans and improves the ecology of our environment. The cartridge has a diameter of about 0.32″ (82 mm) and a height of about 0,3″ (74 mm), its weight is about 0,077 lbs (35 grams).

  • What sizes of dogs can the device be used for?

    DOCLawn is suitable for collecting dog waste regardless of the size of the dogs. The capacity limit is limited by the volume of the cartridge. It is possible to use one cartridge for sequential simultaneous collection of waste for several dogs. As practical tests have shown one cartridge holds the waste of two or three medium-sized dogs 20-27″ (50-70 cm) high per one walk.

  • Can I keep a used replacement waste cartridge for a long time?

    The device is designed in such a way that you can throw the replacement cartridge along with the collected waste into the nearest trash can while walking the dog immediately after use.

  • Will the device be clean after use?

    Once applied, DOCLawn will stay clean. The height of the replacement cartridge is greater than the average height of the waste above the surface. The side walls of the device may get a little dirty when used in very tall grass. If necessary, you can use a cleaning pad to clean the DOCLawn.

  • What surfaces can I use the device on?

    DOCLawn is designed to operate on mowed lawns, however it will perform its task on tall grass, snow, asphalt, sidewalk and sand. In this case, it may be necessary to increase the duration of using the device in the mode of rotation of the cartridge. The main condition of performance is the absence of disorder in the dog. DOCLawn will not be able to collect liquid waste well if the dog has soft stool. There are also no restrictions on the use of DOCLawn indoors.

  • How many replacement cartridges are there in the box, and what is the size?

    The box 10x10x20″ (0.25x0.24x0.58 meters) holds 63 cartridges, that provides a stock of just over one month with cleaning up of dog waste twice per day. The box weighs approximately 5.7 lbs or 2.6 kg.

  • Why is there a different number of cartridges in the same size boxes, 56 or 63?

    In order to reduce the delivery cost, it is more advantageous to send DOCLawn inside the box with cartridges rather than in a separate parcel. It takes up a certain volume, which is 7 cartridges.

  • Is the device waterproof?

    The use of DOCLawn does not imply submersion of the entire device in water, however, rain, snow or splashing water will not harm it or damage electronic components due to the special design of the case. There are also no restrictions on the use of DOCLawn in the rain, only the replacement cartridge will need to be protected from long-term exposure to water, because it will lose its rigidity.

  • How easily can it break down?

    Unlikely. The device is designed with a margin of safety in both the case and the electronics. Controlling and using DOCLawn is so simple and straightforward that even a child can handle it. The electrical circuit contains automatic protection of the electric motor against overloads, as well as protection of batteries against improper charging.

  • Can the device shock me?

    No, it can’t. The largest voltage inside the DOCLawn is 12 V. The only electronic element that a person will need to touch directly is a control button with an operating voltage of 3.3 V. The rest of the electronics are inside the case.

  • How often do I need to recharge the batteries?

    This parameter depends on the duration of the flashlight use and the rotation time of the DOCLawn motors. Tests have shown that when using the device in normal mode twice a day, recharging will be required once every 3-4 weeks. The batteries are recharged via a conventional telephone charger (110-220V) of the USB Type-C standard (not included in the delivery set). To recharge, you will need to remove the cover or telescopic handle. The battery charge status signaling is realized by means of the control button illumination as follows: short blinking - it is necessary to recharge the battery; long blinking - the charging is in progress; constant lit and extinguished backlight - the battery is charged.

  • Is the device certified?

    Yes. DOCLawn has been successfully certified for the requirements of TR CU 020/2011 "Electromagnetic compatibility of technical equipment". This is a complete analogue of Directive 2014/30/ЕU EMC. The health and safety of sponsors is all important to us.

  • What colors are available to order?

    DOCLawn is currently only available in black or swamp green or a combination of these colors with various mixes

  • What is included with a DOCLawn purchase?

    The standard set includes DOCLawn itself, a replaceable telescopic handle and 56 cartridges. In addition, depending on the order, you can also order extra box of replacement cartridges. Batteries and a charger are not included in the package.

  • Can this be used in winter?

    DOCLawn can be used in all seasons. With prolonged exposure to cold, a slight decrease in the capacity of the batteries is possible, which will entail more frequent recharging.

  • How bright does the flashlight shine?

    Due to its position on the lower circumference of the DOCLawn, the flashlight illuminates the cleaning area very well. There is enough lighting to illuminate a large area. The electronic circuit is designed so that the luminescence intensity does not depend on the state of charge of the batteries. The flashlight will always shine the same. Switching on / switching off occurs by long pressing of the button.

  • What country will produce DOCLawn?

    Small part of DOCLawn's electronic components will be ordered in Asia, most of the parts themselves and the final assembly will be made in Belarus. In the future, the production of replacement cartridges will be localized in local markets and they can easily be bought at the nearest pet store.

  • Are VAT, customs duties and fees included in the price?

    The price does not include VAT, customs duties, and import fees from your country. Depending on where you live, backers are responsible for paying these fees themselves.

  • Will I be able to buy DOCLawn anywhere outside of Kickstarter or later after the campaign ends?

    For about 30 days after the conclusion of the crowdfunding campaign, DOCLawn will still be available to order on our website doclawn.com. Only after sending DOCLawn to our backers and fulfilling all orders received from the site we will start selling DOCLawn elsewhere.
